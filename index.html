FAQ

In case you
missed anything.

What is Vera?

Vera is a decentralized protocol built on top of major blockchains that allows essential financial services for NFTs such as renting, lending, and mortgages. Decentralized protocols are non-custodial, meaning the services never rely on the custody of any middleman or intermediary.

How does NFT renting or lending work?

Lending and renting allows you to list your NFT on the marketplace with your desired rental terms. Collateralized renting requires the borrower to deposit collateral to rent your NFT while non-collateralized renting does not require any collateral. Currently, our testnet supports collateralized lending and renting. Non-collateralized lending and renting will be available later in Q4 2021.

How does NFT financing or mortgages work?

There are two types of NFT financing or mortgages: seller financing and network financing. Seller financing allows you to purchase an NFT with a down payment today and you can pay the remaining amount by a deadline specified by the seller without the need to deposit collateral. Network financing allows you to purchase an NFT with a loan from Vera, and you will pay Vera back the loan plus interest over time.

Are there any risks for my NFTs to be stolen?

NFT rentals that require collateral can be stolen. If that occurs, the lender gains the collateral which is intended to deter theft. Rentals that do not require collateral cannot be stolen because Vera's Seller Protection prevents the NFT from leaving the renter's wallet during the entire duration of the rental. On the other hand, NFT mortgages cannot be stolen even if the borrower's payments default thanks to Vera's Seller Protection technology.

Are there any hidden fees or charges?

When you rent an NFT, there are no additional fees or charges on top of your rent. Vera will take a small one-time % service fee from the lender based on the total cost of the rental. When you require a mortgage to buy an NFT, there are no additional fees or charges on top of principle plus interest you agree to pay the lender. Vera will take a small one-time % service fee from the seller based on the total revenue from the NFT sale.
